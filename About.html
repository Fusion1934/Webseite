About Me

I am a User Experience Design student at technische Hochschule Ingolstadt with a strong love for designing, implementing and testing interfaces for humans. My work combines UX thinking with hands-on development in web technologies, interactive prototyping, and 3D design.

I am deeply fascinated by the intricacies of designing across cultures. I believe that every design is inherently rooted in a specific cultural context, which has led me to focus strongly on multicultural projects throughout my work.
